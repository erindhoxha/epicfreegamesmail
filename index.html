What is EpicFreeGames?

EpicFreeGames is a service that sends you an email every time a new free game is available on the Epic Games Store.

How it works?

Just by subscribing you will already start receiving emails weekly for the free games available on the Epic Games Store.

You will be able to unsubscribe at any time.
